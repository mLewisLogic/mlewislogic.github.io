A few weeks ago, I witnessed my buddy Chad attempt to take on two &#8220;Triple Atomic Challenges,&#8221; back to back, at a Quaker Steak and Lube. These wings are so hot that you have to sign a waiver just to order them, and eating an entire order of 5 gets your name on the wall. We bet Chad $50 that he couldn&#8217;t eat 10, with nothing to drink but Bud Light.

For some background, these wings are made with ghost chilis and measure 500,000 Scoville units [[wikipedia][1]] of heat. For comparison, A jalapeño pepper measures 2,000-8,000 Scovilles. Triple Atomic wings are seriously hot.

Rather than spoil the whole thing, an edited video of his attempt can be seen here. Trust me, it&#8217;s well worth the 11 minutes to watch the whole thing.
