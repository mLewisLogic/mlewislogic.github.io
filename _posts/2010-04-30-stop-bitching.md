Seriously. This post isn't to bitch about people bitching. This is simply a challenge.

&nbsp;

Make a commitment to yourself to never complain. Ever. You are in control of your life. *You are in your current position by your decisions alone*. No matter how slighted we may feel at times, we made a decision along the way that placed us in that position. Save that energy that you would have spent bitching, and instead fix the problem.

Change your situation, learn to avoid pitfalls in the future, be more aware of the life currents that surround you. Whenever you complain, you give up control and sound like you just want someone else to clean your shit up for you. Everyone else is busy fixing their own fuckups. Time to take responsibility for yours.

&nbsp;

Learn, grow, and come to avoid the negative situations that used to make you want to bitch and complain.
