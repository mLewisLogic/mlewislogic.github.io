<img class="alignright" src="http://us.movember.com/uploads/images/Home/Mo%20Space/Mo%20Logo%20Stacked%20Small.jpg" alt="Movember Logo" width="187" height="197" />**What the hell is a Movember?!?**
Movember, the month formerly known as November, will be upon us shortly. Movember is a global movement that is bringing much needed attention to cancers that affect men.  I&#8217;m supporting this by growing a Moustache this Movember. My commitment is to start clean-shaven Nov. 1 and grow a moustache all November. I&#8217;m doing this to change the face of men&#8217;s health and I want you to join me.

**
Sounds interesting, but why all the fuss?**
The funds we raise during our Moustache journey go to the Prostate Cancer Foundation and the Lance Armstrong Foundation (LIVESTRONG). What many people don&#8217;t know is that **1 in 6 men** will be diagnosed with prostate cancer in their lifetime and that testicular cancer is the most common cancer in men aged 18-35.  Facts like these have convinced me I should get involved.

You can learn more about Movember by watching the Intro Video at: [http://us.movember.com][1].

**
This sound great!!! How can I help?**

If you don&#8217;t want to (or can&#8217;t!) grow a Mo, please consider making a donation via <http://us.movember.com/mospace/183047>.
